Top findings (example)

Demo Output
1
Hero promise is feature-led, not outcome-led
"Powerful dashboards" tells visitors what it is. Rewrite to lead with what changes for them — e.g. "Cut reporting time in half."
Impact: High · Effort: Low
2
Primary CTA appears below the fold on mobile
Move "Start free trial" above the fold and reduce the navigation height on small screens.
Impact: High · Effort: Low
3
No social proof near the pricing decision
Add a single, specific quote or a logo strip next to pricing tiers to reduce hesitation at the decision moment.
Impact: Medium · Effort: Low
4
Hero image is 1.4MB unoptimised
Compress to WebP under 200KB and lazy-load below-the-fold media to improve LCP.
Impact: Medium · Effort: Low

Northwind Studio is a fictional brand used to illustrate our audit format. Your real audit will use your actual page.
